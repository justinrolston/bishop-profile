Faith & engineering, held together

I’m a Senior Software Engineering Manager with a heart for serving God and His people. Across 15+ years at Maranatha Community Church I’ve served as elder, deacon, teacher, community group leader, and youth director — and the nickname “Bishop” grew out of that calling to servant leadership.

I believe technology is a gift meant to build up the body of Christ. When I step away from the keyboard, you’ll usually find me on a run, clearing my head one mile at a time.

How I lead engineers

I lead engineers the way I’d want to be led — with clarity, trust, and a bias for shipping. My work centers on building high-performing teams, making sound architectural calls, and delivering complex systems end to end, from design through full-stack execution.

What I care about most is mentorship: creating the kind of environment where both people and the software they build are given room to flourish.
